# IMC Prosperity 4 — Results

Headline results from **IMC Prosperity 4**, a global algorithmic-trading competition run by IMC Trading. Each round, teams submit a Python trading algorithm to a simulated exchange (scored on P&L) alongside a manual-trading puzzle. Competed solo as **Team HCQS**.

## Scale of the field

| Teams | Players | Universities | Countries |
|------:|--------:|-------------:|----------:|
| 18,803 | 30,703 | 1,549 | 117 |

## Headlines

- **1st worldwide — Round 1 manual trading** (+87,995), out of 18,803 teams.
- **Peak overall rank: 819 / 18,803 (top ~5%)** across 30,703 players in 117 countries.
- **Best algorithmic round: 422nd globally** (Round 2, top ~2%).
- **Round 4 manual: 183rd** — chooser-option static-replication arbitrage ("Vanilla Just Isn't Exotic Enough").

## Per-round results

Round PnL = Algorithmic + Manual. "Overall position" is the global leaderboard rank shown that round.

| Round | Overall position | Round PnL | Algo (rank) | Manual (rank) |
|------:|-----------------:|----------:|-------------|---------------|
| 1 | **819** / 18,803 | 185,518 | +97,523 (1118th) | +87,995 (**1st**) |
| 2 | 2,881 / 18,803 | 122,383 | +98,150 (**422nd**) | +24,233 (736th) |
| 3 | 1,758 / 18,803 | 81,586 | +17,844 (1335th) | +63,742 (669th) |
| 4 | 1,347 / 18,803 | 51,527 | −20,123 (2386th) | +71,650 (183rd) |

Did not enter Round 5 (stepped out before the basket-asset round).

## Honest context

- Overall rank drifted down R1→R4 (819 → 1,347) as the field's algos improved; the algo book turned negative in R4 (−20,123), offset by strong manual (183rd).
- Manual results were consistently strong (1st, 736th, 669th, 183rd); algorithmic peaked at 422nd.
